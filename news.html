Al Ahly Wins Egyptian Super Cup Against Zamalek!

Date: October 24, 2024

Al Ahly emerged victorious against their rivals, Zamalek, in the Egyptian Super Cup. The match ended in a 0-0 draw, and Al Ahly claimed the trophy with a thrilling 7-6 win in the penalty shootout.

Women's Handball Team Secures Bronze Medal

Date: October 19, 2024

Our women’s handball team secured the bronze medal in the African Club Championship with a 31-22 victory over Congo’s AS Otoho. The Eagles delivered a strong performance, leading 16-10 at the end of the first half. They maintained control in the second half, sealing the win and the bronze medal with a final score of 31-22. Our young goalkeeper Gihad Wael was named player of the match for her outstanding performance.

Al Ahly Welcomes Young Africans President

Date: February 29, 2024

Mr. Hersi A.Said, the President of Tanzania's Young Africans SC, paid a visit to our headquarters in Gezira ahead of tomorrow's CAF Champions League encounter between Al Ahly and Young Africans. Dr. Saad Shalaby, the club’s executive director, welcomed the delegation and conveyed greetings from Mahmoud Al Khatib, the club’s president. Dr. Shalaby emphasized the depth of relations between Egypt and Tanzania, as well as the strong ties between Al Ahly and Young Africans SC.

Al Ahly's Board of Directors Visits Zamalek SC

Date: February 19, 2024

Mahmoud El Khatib, the club’s president, and the board of directors paid a visit to Zamalek SC as part of the historic and strong ties between the two clubs. El Khatib expressed his appreciation for the warm welcome, reflecting the deep historical ties between Al Ahly and Zamalek.
